📚 Clear Skin Blueprint

Quick-Reference Infographic • Your Cheat Sheet for Clear Skin

⏱ Daily Skincare Routine

☀️ Morning

  1. Gentle cleanser
  2. Vitamin C serum or niacinamide
  3. Lightweight moisturizer
  4. Sunscreen SPF 30+

🌙 Evening

  1. Oil cleanser / micellar water
  2. Water-based cleanser
  3. Exfoliant (2-3x/week)
  4. Retinoid or treatment
  5. Moisturizer

🧪 Key Ingredients

💧

Salicylic Acid

Unclogs pores • 0.5-2%

💥

Benzoyl Peroxide

Kills bacteria • 2.5-5%

⭐

Retinoids

Gold standard • Cell turnover

💪

Niacinamide

Anti-inflammatory • 2-10%

🍋

Glycolic Acid

Exfoliates surface • AHA

🌱

Azelaic Acid

Fades dark spots • 10-20%

🔍 Types of Acne

Mild

Blackheads

Open comedones, dark from oxidation

Mild

Whiteheads

Closed comedones, small bumps

Moderate

Papules & Pustules

Red bumps, sometimes with pus

Severe

Nodules & Cysts

Deep, painful — see a dermatologist

🍴 Diet Tips

✅ Skin-Friendly Foods

  • Salmon & sardines (omega-3)
  • Leafy greens & berries
  • Pumpkin seeds (zinc)
  • Green tea (antioxidants)
  • Probiotic-rich foods

❌ Potential Triggers

  • Sugar & high-glycemic foods
  • Dairy (especially skim milk)
  • Whey protein supplements
  • Processed / fried foods
  • Excess alcohol

🛠️ Daily Habits

💤

Sleep 7-9 Hours

Skin repairs overnight. Poor sleep raises cortisol.

💧

Drink 8 Glasses Water

Hydrated skin produces less excess oil.

🧗

Exercise Regularly

Boosts circulation. Shower after sweating.

😌

Manage Stress

Meditation, deep breathing, journaling.

📱

Clean Your Phone

Wipe screen daily to avoid transferring bacteria.

🛌

Fresh Pillowcase

Change every 2-3 days or use silk.

⚠️
Don't: Pop cysts, over-wash your face, use too many actives at once, skip sunscreen, or expect overnight results. Most treatments take 6-12 weeks.
💡
Do: Keep a skin diary, introduce products one at a time, patch test everything, and see a dermatologist if OTC treatments haven't worked after 12 weeks.
